Taco Recipe

Description:

Simple, bold, and built for flavor. These tacos use minimal toppings to let the seasoned meat shine, just like you’d find from a taco truck in the heart of Texas.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb skirt steak or flank steak (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tbsp oil (for searing)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • ¼ cup diced white onion
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Salsa verde

Equipment:

  • Cast iron skillet or grill pan
  • Tongs
  • Cutting board & knife
  • Small bowls for toppings

Instructions:

  1. Prep the steak
    Season sliced steak with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temp for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook the meat
    Heat oil in skillet over high heat. Sear steak until browned and just cooked through (about 3–4 minutes).
  3. Warm tortillas
    Heat tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over flame until soft and slightly charred.
  4. Assemble tacos
    Fill tortillas with steak, then top with onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Serving Tips

Serve with chilled Mexican Coke or agua fresca.
