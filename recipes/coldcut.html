Sandwich Recipe

Description:

This sandwich layers tender honey roasted turkey with crisp veggies and a trio of sauces that bring heat, creaminess, and tang. American cheese melts into the meat, while cucumbers and jalapeños add crunch and kick. It’s a deli-style masterpiece with a spicy twist and perfect for lunch, dinner, or impressing your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 4–6 slices of roasted turkey breast
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced red onions
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced cucumbers
  • 2 tbsp pickled jalapeños (adjust to taste)
  • 1 leaf romaine or iceberg lettuce
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (plus extra if you’re feeling it)
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp chipotle mayo
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 slices of toasted sandwich bread (sourdough, wheat, or brioche work great)

Equipment:

  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Butter knife or spreader
  • Toaster or skillet

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread
    Lightly toast both slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Layer the sauces
    Spread mayo on one slice, chipotle mayo on the other, and a thin layer of mustard on either side.
  3. Build the base
    Lay down the turkey slices and top with American cheese.
  4. Add the crunch
    Layer cucumbers, onions, and jalapeños over the cheese.
  5. Top it off
    Add lettuce, then season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Assemble and press
    Close the sandwich, press gently, and slice diagonally for presentation.

Serving Tips

Serve with kettle chips or a pickle spear for extra crunch.
